PRODUCT PHOTOS — drop your real crystal-ball images here.

The "Get Yours Tonight" section (and its design thumbnails) shows these files.
Until a file exists, that design falls back to the built-in CSS crystal ball.

Required filenames (save exactly like this, JPG):

  moon.jpg    → the Moon crystal ball photo
  solar.jpg   → the Solar System crystal ball photo
  shiva.jpg   → the Lord Shiva crystal ball photo

Tips:
- Roughly square images look best (they're shown in a square frame, centered).
- Keep each file under ~300 KB (≈800–1000px wide) for fast mobile loading.
- PNG/WEBP also work, but then update the `image:` paths in /lib/designs.js
  to match the extension (e.g. "/products/moon.webp").
